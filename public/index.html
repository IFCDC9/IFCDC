About IFCDC

The Imperial Foundation Community Development Center (IFCDC) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to strengthening the Asbury Park community and surrounding areas. Under the leadership of Mr. Fahreal Allah, Owner & Executive Director, IFCDC delivers integrated programs that address the full spectrum of community needs.

From mental health and wellness to youth development, workforce readiness, and neighborhood revitalization, IFCDC serves as a central hub for resources, support, and community connection.
