Records & Privacy Policy

The Imperial Foundation Community Development Center (IFCDC) protects all mental health, medical, and wellness-related records with the highest level of confidentiality.

Executive Custodian

All protected health information is managed under the authority of: Mr. Fahreal Allah – Owner & Executive Director. No records are shared, released, or accessed without his approval.

Client Rights

Confidentiality

Your information is confidential and protected under applicable privacy laws and IFCDC policy.

Access to Records

You may request a copy of your records at any time by contacting our office.

Authorization to Share

You may authorize IFCDC to share your information with another provider or agency of your choosing.

Right to Revoke

You may revoke any authorization to share your records at any time by submitting a written request.

Release of Information (ROI)

A Release of Information (ROI) authorization will be required for any records request. All requests must be approved by the Executive Director before any records are shared.

Processing time is typically 5–10 business days after approval.

What Information Is Protected

  • Mental health assessments and treatment records
  • Medical and wellness documentation
  • Intake forms and personal history
  • Case notes and progress documentation
  • Referral and coordination records
  • Any information shared during counseling or support sessions

Exceptions to Confidentiality

In limited circumstances, IFCDC may be required to disclose information without your consent, including:

  • Imminent danger to yourself or others
  • Suspected abuse or neglect of a child, elder, or dependent adult
  • Court orders or legal subpoenas
  • As otherwise required by law

In such cases, IFCDC will make every effort to limit disclosure to only the information required.
