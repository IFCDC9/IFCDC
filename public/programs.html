IFCDC Programs

Comprehensive community programs designed to support, stabilize, and strengthen individuals and families across Asbury Park and surrounding areas.

Youth & Education

Youth Mentorship

One-on-one and group mentoring for young people ages 10–24, focusing on life skills, academic support, and positive decision-making.

Anti-Gang & Violence Prevention

Street outreach, conflict mediation, and trauma-informed support to interrupt violence and provide alternatives.

Scholarship Program

Financial support for education and training for community members pursuing academic and vocational goals.

After-School Support

Safe spaces and structured activities for youth during after-school hours, including tutoring and enrichment.

Adult & Family Services

Workforce Development

Job readiness training, resume building, interview prep, and connections to local employers.

Transitional Housing

Support for individuals and families navigating housing instability, with case management and wraparound services.

Economic Development

Small business support, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship resources for community members.

Family Support Services

Parenting support, family mediation, and resources to strengthen family stability.

Community & Wellness

Mental Health & Wellness

Comprehensive behavioral health support for all ages. Learn more →

IFCDC Radio

Community radio programming featuring local voices, music, and neighborhood news.

IFCDC Barbershop

A community hub offering haircuts, mentorship, and a safe space for connection.

Senior Services

Programs addressing social isolation, wellness activities, and support for older adults.
